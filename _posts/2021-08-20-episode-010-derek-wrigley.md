Derek Wrigley was a highly significant designer and design champion. A people
focused designer.
Derek was a pioneering designer, architect, teacher, author, photographer,
sculptor, inventor, engineer, manufacturer, innovator and philosopher. A
renaissance man, in fact.

In this conversation Derek speak about his life experience and mentions many
notable friends such as Fred Ward, Ross Honin, Essington Lewis, Australian
National University Design Unit, Gerald Easden, Ian Howard, Harry Sebel, Alister
Morrison, Charles Furey, Gordon Andrew, George Kral, Walter Gropius, Mies van
der Rohe, Arthur Robinson, Hans Pillig, Roy Grounds, Sir John Eccles, and Sir
Mark Oliphant.
